TRENDY

In this game, a randomly generated question is formed from google trends data with random answers. The questions are based off of google searches from around the world and from the years 2004 to 2022. Your job is to select which search is the most popular out of the three other choices.

You have 30 seconds to answer as many questions as possible. Each question is worth 1 point. If you get the correct answer the timer adds three more seconds. If you get the wrong answer the timer removes 5 seconds. After the time runs out, the game is over.
